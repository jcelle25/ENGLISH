Lines & Graphs in Technical Writing

Definition

Lines and graphs are visual tools used to present data clearly and concisely. They make information easier to understand and compare.

Line Graph

Shows trends or changes over time.

Bar Graph

Used to compare quantities across categories.

Pie Chart

Used to show proportions of a whole.

Tips for Using Graphs

  • Always label axes and units clearly.
  • Use consistent colors for clarity.
  • Include a descriptive title for each graph.
  • Keep graphs simple and easy to read.
